Frequently asked questions

What will happen after I sign up?

After you’ve registered your property, we will review your information to make sure we have everything we need. You’ll then receive an email with login details for your Extranet. This is where you’ll go to update your availability and prices. After that you’ll receive instructions on how to set your property live on Stayhopper Mobile App and Web site.

Open and close your property when you want?

We give you the freedom to open or close your property on our site whenever you want. With no lock-in contracts, you're in control of your property. Your peace of mind is our top priority.
