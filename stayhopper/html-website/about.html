With Stayhopper, we are introducing an innovative concept of Micro-stay to break through the conventional pattern of hotel bookings. Whether you want to venture out to explore the city for a few hours during your transit or get a quick nap after your busy schedules of business meetings, you can now book your stay by the hours and save big.

We are a bunch of travellers ourselves, and we have all been there.
